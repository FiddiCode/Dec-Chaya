    Things to do around Ranikhet

    Rani Jheel

     
    Rani Jheel is a popular attraction in Ranikhet. It features an artificial lake and a small walkway that you can use to walk over the river. There is also a rope slide that you can use to get across the river. Come, enjoy a visit to this beautiful destination in Ranikhet. 

    Haidakhan Babaji Temple

     
    This temple is set amidst picturesque settings and the calm environment only adds to the overall beauty of this place. This temple is very well maintained and it also features a small ashram that is present within the premises. 
     

    Mankameshwar Temple

     

    Mankameshwar is one of the most famous temples in Ranikhet. This temple was constructed by the Kumaon regiment of the Indian army. This temple exudes spirituality and should be on your itinerary during your next trip to Ranikhet. 

    Jhula Temple

     
    Jhula Temple is an 8th-century temple that is present in Ranikhet. It doubles up as both a spiritual and historical destination and people from all over the country visit this place during the course of the year.

    Majhkali

     
    Majhkali is a small hamlet that is located about 12 km from Ranikhet and is known for its natural beauty and its numerous picnic spots. This venue also features splendid views of the snow-capped Himalayas and a temple of Goddess Kali that travellers can visit during their trip to Majhkali.

    Zip-lining in Ranikhet

     
    Ranikhet offers a wide range of adventurous outdoor activities, one of them being zip-lining. Our hotel is located in close proximity to many zip-lining locations. Visit Ranikhet today and indulge in this activity to get your adrenaline pumping!
